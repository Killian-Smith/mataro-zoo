About Us

Mataró Zoo

Mataró Zoo is located in Mataró, Spain, and is one of Spain's most popular zoos! The zoo opened in 1892 and has over 2,000 animals and is located on 32 acres of land. There are many types of animals, including lions, tigers, bears, monkeys, and many more. The zoo is open every day of the year from 10:00am to 6:00 pm except for Christmas, on that day it is closed.

Featured Animals

Giraffe

The giraffe is a large African hoofed mammal belonging to the genus Giraffa. It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth.

Chimpanzee

The chimpanzee, also known as simply the chimp, is a species of great ape native to the forest and savannah of tropical Africa. It has four confirmed subspecies and a fifth proposed subspecies.

Turtle

Turtles are an order of reptiles known as Testudines, characterized by a shell developed mainly from their ribs. Modern turtles are divided into two major groups, the Pleurodira and Cryptodira, which differ in the way the head retracts.
